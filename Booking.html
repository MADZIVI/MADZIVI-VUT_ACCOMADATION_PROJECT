Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the booking process take?

Our team reviews all booking requests within 24 hours and will contact you with confirmation or additional information requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept NSFAS transfers, cash payments, EFT transfers, and monthly installments. You can select your preferred method during the booking process.

Can I cancel my booking?

Cancellation policies depend on your specific lease agreement. Please contact us directly to discuss cancellation options for your accommodation.

Are deposits required?

Yes, a security deposit is typically required upon booking confirmation. The amount varies depending on the accommodation type.

Can families book accommodation?

Our platform is primarily for university students, but we encourage parents to contact us directly to discuss specific situations.

How do I verify my accommodation is legitimate?

All accommodations listed on our platform have been verified and checked by our Provider Verification Specialist. Always get a written lease agreement.
